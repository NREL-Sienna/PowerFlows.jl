display(sys) reveals that this system has 5 thermal generators and 3 fixed loads. We could go check their names, locations, set points with the various getters...but for that, refer to the PowerSystems.jl tutorial.

Step 2: Define the Power Flow

Initialize a power flow solver. Here, we'll highlight two options, ACPowerFlow and DCPowerFlow

julia> pf_ac = ACPowerFlow()ACPowerFlow{NewtonRaphsonACPowerFlow}(false, nothing, false, false, nothing, true, false, false)
julia> pf_dc = DCPowerFlow()DCPowerFlow(nothing)

There's also PTDFDCPowerFlow and vPTDFDCPowerFlow.

The load's active power and its max active power are both 3 (per unit). Let's increase both of those numbers by 10%:

julia> set_max_active_power!(load_2, get_max_active_power(load_2) * 1.10)3.3000000000000003
julia> set_active_power!(load_2, get_active_power(load_2) * 1.10)3.3000000000000003
